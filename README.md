## Projects

### AgriLink
> Reducing post-harvest fruit spoilage through real-time IoT monitoring, SMS alerts, and handling quality transparency.

A spoilage-prevention and supply chain transparency system for smallholder fruit farmers in the Philippines. Targets farmers storing fruit-bearing crops such as durian, mango, rambutan, and lanzones.

- IoT sensors (ESP32 + SHT31) for real-time temperature and humidity monitoring
- SMS alerts requiring no smartphone
- Handling Quality Score for buyer transparency
- B2B marketplace for verified produce batches

---

### Airline Reservation and Ticketing System
> A web-based platform for automating airline reservations, ticketing, and administrative operations.

Developed under **Omnira** as part of CCC181. A full-stack system covering flight search, PNR booking, secure payment processing, e-ticket issuance, and an admin management portal.

- Passenger booking with automated PNR creation and seat selection
- Secure payment processing with e-ticket generation
- Admin portal for flight schedule and booking management
- Role-based access control and data encryption

---

### Predicting Educational Underperformance in the Philippines
> A machine learning approach to NAT 2023–2024 regional disparities.

Applied three supervised machine learning models to 6,636 school-level NAT records across five Philippine regions to predict school performance tiers and identify subject-level predictors of underperformance.

- Models: Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest
- Decision Tree and Logistic Regression achieved perfect accuracy (1.0000)
- Mathematics identified as the strongest predictor (r = 0.814)
- Region IX flagged as the lowest-performing region (mean MPS: 53.79%)
